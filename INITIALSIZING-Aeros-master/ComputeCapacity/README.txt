--------------- Airplane Check --------------------------

In order to check the viability of an airplane the range desirable range needs to be defined.

The program will compute the number and mass of the propulsion group and the needed capacity and mass of the batteries
A realistic Mass_Batteris / MTOW is more than 0.25 %
